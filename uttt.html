Instructions
Ultimate Tic Tac Toe is an expansion on normal Tic Tac Toe that features 9 smaller boards arranged into a single large board.

Players take turns placing their pieces in one of the spaces of a small board, the location of which determines which small board their opponent can play in next.

When a small board is won, it becomes a filled in piece in the large board for the winner. A player wins the overall game when they manage to get a line of 3 on the large board.

Extreme Ultimate Tic Tac Toe is an expansion on normal Ultimate Tic Tac Toe that features special items that spawn in spaces randomly at the beginning of the game. These items have the following special effects:

Bomb - Allows the next player to play on any small board.
Magnet - Forces the next player to play on the same small board.
Wildcard - Counts as a piece for either player. Can be played on top of.
